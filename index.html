Manifeste

Les Champs Magnétiques is a conceptual fashion house inspired by Surrealist pioneers André Breton and Philippe Soupault, who published Les Champs Magnétiques in 1920. Their literary experiment, blending instinct and intention, chaos and control, continues to resonate a century later.

Our house explores these contradictions, crafting garments and objects of desire that are provocative yet wearable, conceptual yet approachable. Each collection stakes a claim in the broader conversation of art and fashion, inviting wearers to navigate a shifting world with curiosity and purpose.
